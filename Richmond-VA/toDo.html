Hardywood Park Craft Brewery

Address: 2410 Ownby Ln, Richmond, VA 23220

Hardywood provides an outdoor beer garden with corn hole and bocce ball available. they also offer an eclectic mix of live music and community events that are free to the public and open to all ages regardless of it being a brewery! Hardywood is only one of the dozen breweries surrounding the Richmond area.

Ages: All (kids of course can't drink but they can vibe to the music and events)

Children's Museum of Richmond Central

Address: 2626 West Broad Street Richmond, VA 23220

Bringing the whole family out for break? A visit to the Children’s Museum could just be the right fit for family activities. It isn't just fun. It’s an opportunity for the whole family to play and learn together. From fundamental concepts to socialization skills, language development, and much much more!

Children & Adults (Ages 1 - 59): $9.00

Under 1 year old: FREE

Seniors: $8.00

Ages: All

The James River / Belle Isle

Address: 300 Tredegar St., Richmond, VA 23219

In which other city could you go kayaking or rafting through an urban setting? The James River isn't just a place that shoots rapids. Belle Isle is a small island that lies between the James River which is a popular hang out spot where most locals have been. Picnics, Tanning, Rock Hopping, and fishing are all just of the few of the many activities to do & its family friendly for all ages!

Admission: FREE

Ages: All
